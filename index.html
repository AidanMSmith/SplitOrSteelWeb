How To Play


Basics

Each player is given a choice to either split or steel, depending on their preference players will either split or attempt to steal the prize from the other player.



Tactics

Oftentimes, if possible, the other player will attempt to steal the prize from you, depending on their dice roll. In such case it is better to steel than to split the other player.

PowerUps


Hijack

Hijack will steal the enemies dice roll and will use it against them.



Unstoppable Force

Adds +3 to current diceroll.

Destiny

Depending on players turn, will show enemy's diceroll. If player is first to go, their turn will be delayed until other players turn.
